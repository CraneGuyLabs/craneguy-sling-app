# Privacy Policy — CraneGuyLabs Sling App

Effective Date: 2026

CraneGuyLabs (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in connection with the CraneGuyLabs Sling App (the “Application”).

---

## Overview

CraneGuyLabs Sling App is a professional, below-the-hook rigging calculator designed to operate without collecting personal data from users.

The Application does **not** require user accounts, does **not** track users, and does **not** collect analytics or usage data.

---

## Information We Collect

### Crash Data (Diagnostics Only)

The Application collects **crash logs only**, for the sole purpose of identifying and fixing software errors.

Crash data may include:
- Application crash stack traces  
- App version  
- Operating system version  
- Device model  

This data is:
- Collected **only when the app crashes**
- Used **only** to improve app stability and reliability
- **Not linked** to a user’s identity
- **Not used** for tracking, profiling, or advertising

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

The Application does **not** collect, store, or transmit:
- Names, email addresses, or contact information  
- Location data  
- User inputs or calculation data  
- Usage analytics or behavioral metrics  
- Advertising identifiers  
- Tracking data of any kind  

---

## How Information Is Used

Crash data is used solely to:
- Diagnose software defects
- Improve application reliability
- Address stability issues that could affect safe use

Crash data is **not** used for analytics, marketing, advertising, or user profiling.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share user data.

Crash data may be processed by a crash-reporting service provider strictly for diagnostic purposes and only as necessary to identify and resolve software issues.

---

## Data Retention

Crash data is retained only for as long as necessary to diagnose and resolve the associated issue.

---

## Children’s Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Continued use of the Application after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
